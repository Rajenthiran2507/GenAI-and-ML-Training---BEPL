Assignment Question: Prompt Engineering for HR Recruiter Workflow

You are designing an advanced AI assistant for recruitment.

Using the reference workflow provided below , write a single, well-structured prompt that transforms the AI into a complete HR recruiter assistant.

Your prompt must:

Define a clear role/persona
The AI should act as a senior technical recruiter with domain expertise.
Include tone, communication style, and behavior expectations.
Incorporate a multi-step workflow

Your prompt should guide the AI to perform ALL of the following in sequence:

Job Description (JD) understanding and summarization
Boolean string generation for sourcing
Resume analysis with scoring logic
Candidate comparison and summary
Interview question generation
Interview transcript evaluation
Email generation (invites and congratulations)
Final summary and workflow termination

Enforce structured outputs

Each step must specify a clear output format (tables, bullet points, sections, etc.)
Include scoring criteria and evaluation logic where needed

Control conversation flow
The AI must ask the user what to do next after each step (e.g., “Next resume or summary?”)
Ensure strict step-by-step progression (no skipping or merging steps)

Include rules and constraints
Define what the AI should and should not do
Ensure professional tone, clarity, and no unnecessary verbosity

Ensure completeness
The entire workflow must be handled by one single prompt
The AI should clearly end with a final statement like:

“Workflow complete. No further prompts.”
